Our Story

“Oana and Sarah met while pursuing their postgraduate degrees in Entrepreneurship at Trinity College Dublin in 2019. Driven by their shared passion for electric cars and their ambition to make a difference, they founded EVE and have been building it ever since. The team has a shared experience of over 12 years in business development and product management in the automotive and travel tech industries.

In June 2020, they joined LaunchBox, Tangent’s Student Accelerator at Trinity College Dublin. The program allowed them to take the first steps in setting up the foundations of EVE and supported them in creating traction.

Since August 2020, the founding team has joined Class XXVI of Alchemist, a leading Silicon Valley accelerator, which opens new opportunities to realise their vision.”
